Privacy Policy

GeoTag Camera (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

The app collects only the data required for its core functionality:

  • Camera Data: Photos captured using the app
  • Location Data: Precise GPS coordinates (latitude & longitude)
  • Address Data: Reverse-geocoded location information
  • Timestamp: Date and time of capture

2. How We Use Data

All data is used only to:

  • Capture geotagged photos
  • Embed GPS coordinates into images
  • Display location information on photos
  • Allow saving and sharing of images

We do not use your data for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store any user data on external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.

5. Permissions Used

  • Camera: To capture photos
  • Location (Precise): To tag photos with GPS coordinates
  • Media Storage: To save and access images

6. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Security

All processing happens on-device. However, no system is 100% secure.

9. Your Control

  • You can delete photos anytime from your device
  • You can revoke permissions in system settings

10. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Updates will be posted here.
